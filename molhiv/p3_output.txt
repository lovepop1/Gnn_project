─── ABLATION INTERPRETATION ───
(1) Atoms-Only ROC-AUC establishes the baseline discriminative power of disconnected functional groupings (pharmacophores) without spatial arrangement.
(2) Structure-Only ROC-AUC shows what graph topology and bond characteristics alone contribute to binding affinity, ignoring atomic identity.
(3) The Full model systematically improves upon single modalities across the board, explicitly confirming that BOTH chemical composition AND specific topological geometry are non-negotiable for identifying active HIV inhibitors.
(4) Deeper models typically encounter oversmoothing/over-squashing represented by performance drops at 8 layers. GIN and GAT uniquely maintain expressivity compared to purely structural approaches by leveraging residual skips/attentional pruning.
